Procedure:

After you booked your flight and the pilot confirmed that the weather conditions are flyable,

you'll meet with the pilot at the office and you have to fill out a ticket for your flight. Please be punctual, we have a very strict timetable and can't garuante your flight if you are late!

After you filled out your ticket, you'll drive with the pilot to the cable-car and get up to the top of the Schmittenhöhe.

On top the pilot will check the equiment and gives you a short security briefing. Then you are ready to take off and enjoy the view. The pilot will take pictures and a video of the flight.

After your save landing in the field next to the office, we will do the payment of the flight.

Prices & Payment:

Our Prices for the Tandem Flights do not include the price for the cablecar. You can find the current prices here.
